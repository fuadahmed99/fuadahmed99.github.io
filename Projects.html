Product Design and Development IPE3104
Three in one Multipurpose weight Carrier for Construction workers in Bangladesh

June-Oct 2019

A versatile three-in-one multipurpose weight carrier designed for the construction workers of Bangladesh has been developed. This innovative solution is capable of carrying heavy loads, serving as a trolley for easy transportation, and facilitating weightlifting on the head. The design and analysis of this product were meticulously executed using Solidworks software. Moreover, it is constructed from an affordable and environmentally friendly biodegradable material, cane. This multipurpose weight carrier aims to enhance the efficiency and convenience of construction work in Bangladesh while zero environmental impact.

An established industry always avidly tries to find a way to reduce their cost and maximize their profit. In this research two total cost equations have been analyzed and optimized. L9 orthogonal array of Taguchi Analysis was applied on both equations to find out the optimum parameter of the total cost equation.
